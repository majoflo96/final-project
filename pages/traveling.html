GALLERY ABOUT TRAVELING CONTACT
I decided to dedicate a page for my traveling passion. This way I want you to connect with me on a more personal level. My idea is to get photos of myself from all around the world. I want to take a photo with myself on it in every country I've travelled to.

I do miss a lot of photos from a lot of countries because I came up with this idea a little bit too late. Please check it frequently for updates. I might also find some older photos and I will update this page when I do. Of course another way to keep up to date is by following me on Instagram.

Switzerland

I´ve been in Switzerland 5 times. I studied in a boarding school in Montreux when I was younger. My favorite thing to do over there is spend time with nature like hiking in the mountains. Switzerland has one of the most amazing views.

Jordan

Jordan has one of the most beautiful deserts called "Wadi Rum." I´ve been there a couple of times and love doing roadtrips and admiring the beautiful views, especially sunsets.

Lebanon

I visited Lebanon for the fist time in 2019. It´s such a unique country with a very interesting culture. The food is amazing and it has incredible historic sites like Baalbek.

Kenya

I´ve been to Kenya 2 times. In this picture I was in lake Nakuru. It is located in the rift valley of Kenya and it is famous for attracting a vast quantity of flamingos that line in shore.

Tunisia

Tunisia is such a beautiful country with beautiful architecture. I visited for the first time last summer. In this picture I was enjoying the pool at our beautiful boutique hotel located in the island of Djerba.

Portugal

Porto is a fascinating and vibrant city in Portugal. It has beautiful sights and it´s famous for its seafood. I spent a beautiful time with family and friends.

India

India will be forever one of my favorite countries. The culture, the people, the food, the history, everything. I´ve been to India 4 times so far and I never get tired of it. In this picture I visited the ancient wonder of Mathura located in Govardhan.

Philippines

If you love nature like I do you will fall in love with Philippines. There are so many things to do and so many places to see. In this picture I had the chance to swim with whale sharks in Cebu and it was one of the most amazing experiences of my life.
